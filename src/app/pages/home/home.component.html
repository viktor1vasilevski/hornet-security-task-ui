About SpaceX

SpaceX is an American aerospace manufacturer and space transportation company founded by Elon Musk in 2002. The company designs, manufactures, and launches advanced rockets and spacecraft.

SpaceX's mission is to reduce space transportation costs and enable the colonization of Mars.
